[OBJECT:REACTION]unless specified otherwise, assume it's in fort mode.
IRON_ORE_PIKACHU:smelts iron ore into iron bars

USE_DRINKATRON_PLANTBOOZE:converts certain plants to booze
USE_DRINKATRON_PLANTGROWTHBOOZE:converts certain plant parts to booze
USE_DRINKATRON_STONEBOOZE:turns certain rocks into booze
USE_DRINKATRON_HEMA:converts hematite into booze
USE_DRINKATRON_SLADE:converts raw slade into heavy alcohol
